# Importance of Patch Management Report

## Internship Track

Cyber Security

## Task

Task 6 – Research Report: The Importance of Patch Management

## Objective

This project explains the importance of patch management in cybersecurity. It discusses why unpatched systems are vulnerable to cyberattacks and outlines effective patch management strategies that organizations should implement to improve their security posture.


## Topics Covered

- Introduction to patch management
- Why patch management is important
- Risks of unpatched systems
- Common vulnerabilities and exploits
- Patch management best practices
- Recommendations for organizations
- References
